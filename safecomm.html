Cisco Switch Up Challenge

With the growing ubiquity of Internet of Things, the prompt of Cisco’s Switch Up Challenge was to tackle a social or environemental issue by leveraging IoT technologies. After submitting our proposal, our team was invited to present at Cisco London along with other finalists. During this presentation, we presented both a business concept and a working prototype of a feasabile security system for refugee camps. Our product, SafeComm recieved second place.

Presentation

Award Ceremony

Presenting at the Finals in London was a great experiene. We had to opportunity to meet engineering executives as Cisco London as well as other finalists from UK. We were also pleased that SafeComm recieved second place. Everyone on the team felt energized to keep this project going forward after recieving such good feeback on both the feasability and need for the product.
